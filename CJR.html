CRIMINAL JUSTICE

Criminal Justice is a very wide ranging issue, which doesn't stop us from trying our hardest to advocate for every single aspect of it. From successfully lobbying for AB 392, the CA Police of Force Bill, to on campus educational workshops, the Criminal Justice committee is one of the biggest advocates for reform in Northern California.
